Intro

I am an expert in enterprise UX and UI for internal digital tools and B2B solutions, currently focused on the insurance sector. One of my primary responsibilities is collaborating on the development of design systems alongside development teams.

I use Figma as my main tool, with additional support from other tools like Zero Height, Visual Studio, and Chat GPT / Claude for certain projects.

I have worked with a diverse range of clients, with notable projects for Uncovercity, Toyota Spain, Arcano Partners, Kanona Concept, Ibercaja, and currently, exclusively for MAPFRE ACT.

Besides my role at GFT, I am also a designer, host, and DJ at Domingo Club de Baile, a monthly online radio show that features songs from the golden years of funk, disco, and house music.

This is my passion project, where I apply my creativity, communication, and design skills to create a product that celebrates the joy of music and dancing.

CV

GFT Work

As a member of the GFT Design Studio, I specialize in providing interface design solutions for the client MAPFRE and its subsidiaries Tech and Mawdy. My focus is on creating enterprise user experiences, internal tools, and B2B platforms aimed at clients in the insurance sector.

My role spans from conceptualization to implementation, ensuring a cohesive and efficient user experience. Additionally, I participate in the Visual Quality Assurance (Visual QA) process, ensuring that every product meets the highest standards of design, consistency, and functionality.

I actively collaborate in the development and maintenance of the company's Design System, ensuring coherence and efficiency across all projects. I use tools such as Figma, Locofy, Visual Studio, Zero Height, and OpenAI-based applications like Chat GPT to enhance creativity and innovation at every stage of the design process.
